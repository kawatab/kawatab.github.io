Canonのaviからmp4に変換

2014-06-14

$ a="MVI_0995"; vlc -I dummy -vvv "$a.AVI" --sout "#transcode{vcodec=h264,vb=1200,acodec=mp3,ab=128,channels=1,samplerate=11025}:standard{mux=mp4,dst="$a.mp4",access=file}" vlc://quit
